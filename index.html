Privacy Policy

RentStore Manager

Last updated: December 2025

RentStore Manager respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the app.

Information Collection and Use

RentStore Manager does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data. All information entered into the app (such as clients, items, rentals, returns, and payments) is stored locally on your device only.

Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally using the device’s internal database (SQLite).
  • No data is transmitted to external servers.
  • The app works completely offline.

Data Sharing

RentStore Manager does not share any data with third parties. There are no ads, analytics, or tracking services integrated into the app.

Permissions

The app does not require any special permissions. If export features are used, file access is only used to save or share generated files at the user’s request.

User Control

  • You can edit or delete any data directly within the app.
  • Uninstalling the app permanently removes all stored data.

Children’s Privacy

RentStore Manager does not target children and does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
